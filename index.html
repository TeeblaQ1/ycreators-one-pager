About Us

Ycreators is the destination for STEAM education, offering on-site and online programs for kids aged 4 - 17. We are empowering the next generation of future-shapers through STEAM learning. We create transformative life experiences for children through STEAM education and prepare them for the future of work and learning.

Our Creators push beyond the limits of traditional learning aided by our engaging world standard curriculum and gamified hands-on learning experience. Tracks include Coding, Robotics, Arts & Crafts, IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and Oral & Written Communication, Virtual & Augmented Reality, Web 3.0, Quantum Computing, Entrepreneurship, and so much more. This next generation of world-changers is guided by thoroughly vetted, highly skilled instructors in the industry. Their learned skills will ensure they become globally competitive and place them in the top percentile of innovators.

We are on a mission to harness the potential in today’s children and transform them into tomorrow’s Creators.

Our Courses

Programming

Your child will learn to troubleshoot their hardware assembly and then it’s time to boot up the computer—that they will build—to experience the thrill of coding in Scratch, Python, C#, Java, Javascript, Ruby, etc. With an introduction to hardware, engineering, and coding, this course has something for every Creator.


Age Group: 4-6 | 7-9 | 10 - 12 | 13 - 17

Duration: 3 months

3D Design & Printing

Imagination meets manufacturing in this hands-on course where students learn how to design simple 3D models and prepare them for 3D printing. Our Creators learn how to change filaments, maintain 3D printers, and operate them safely.


Age Group: 9 - 12 | 13 - 17

Duration: 3 months

Artificial Intelligence

What if the code your child builds could turn pictures of horses into zebras, tell the difference between cats and dogs, and beat world champions at chess? Step aside sorcery, machine learning is here and performing tasks people used to believe were only possible in fantasy worlds.


Age Group: 4 - 8 | 9 - 12 | 13 - 17

Duration: 3 months

IoT & Smart Devices

IoT is changing the way we live. With more and more devices getting connected to the internet, the demand for skills in this area is very high. Your kids can learn IoT only by building projects – they get their hands dirty with the sensors and actuators, set up the network, and collect & analyze data sent by the sensors.


Age Group: 9 - 12 | 13 - 17

Duration: 3 months

Robotics

Our Creators get hands-on to build their very own autonomous robot. Using basic circuitry and frame components, they’ll construct a base for your robot. Then they’ll add an Arduino, a popular microcontroller for creating new electronic devices, and integrate sensors to let their robot understand and navigate its environment.


Age Group: 4 - 8 | 9 - 12 | 13 - 17

Duration: 3 months

Game Development

Design and build a dynamic game to play on the computer and mobile devices with Anki Cozmo, Code Lab, GameMaker Studio 2, C#, Unity, Microsoft Visual Studio With a user-friendly toolset from GameMaker, behaviors, events, and physics come together to create mobile games, all while teaching the core principles of game design.


Age Group: 4 - 8 | 9 - 12 | 13 - 17

Duration: 3 months

HOW YCREATORS WORK

  • 01 Join Our Program

    Apply today to track your child’s interest. We focus on finding the highest-quality instructors. You can expect to receive your acceptance email within 3 working days. Our seamless and simple onboarding process is designed to give your child a soft landing.
