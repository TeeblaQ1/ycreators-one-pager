About Us

Ycreators is a prep school for continued learning after class. Our mission is to create life-changing experiences with STEAM eduacation that embolden students to shape the future. Our learning experience provides, gamified, hands-on experience. We help our creators push their boundaries by creating an engaging learning curiculum blending Science, Teachnology, Engineering, Arts & Crafts, and Mathematics to explore the creative universe of these young minds.

Our creators learn Artificial Intelligence, Chess, and other fun DIY projects. These activities help our students acquire skills to enable them land dream jobs at world’s iconic comapnies like Google Facebook, Lego, Amazon, General Electric, Epic Games, IBM, Microsoft, etc. We are aware that along the way many will attend ivy league colleges, launch their startups, and create industry distrupting ventures and game-changing non profits.

With our extraordinary passionate and qualified instructors our programs are built to make your child stand out of the crowd and be the best they were born to be.

Our Courses

Programming

Your child will learn to troubleshoot their hardware assembly and then it’s time to boot up the computer—that they will build—to experience the thrill of coding in Python. With an introduction to hardware, engineering, and coding, this course has something for everyone.


Age: 8 - 15

Duration: 3 months

3D Modelling

Imagination meets manufacturing in this hands-on course where students learn how to design simple 3D models and prepare them for 3D printing. Learn how to change the filament, maintain a 3D printer, and operate it safely


Age: 6 - 15

Duration: 3 months

Artificial Intelligence

What if your code could turn pictures of horses into zebras, tell the difference between cats and dogs, and beat world champions at chess? Step aside sorcery, machine learning is here and performing tasks people used to believe we’re only possible in fantasy worlds.


Age: 10 - 15

Duration: 3 months

IoT & Smart Devices

IOT is changing the way we live. With more and more devices getting connected to the internet, the demand for skills in this area is very high. You can learn IOT only by building projects – get your hands on the sensors and actuators, set up the network and collect & analyse data sent by the sensors.


Age: 8 - 15

Duration: 3 months

Robotics

Get hands-on to build your very own autonomous robot. Using basic circuitry and frame components, you’ll construct a base for your robot. Then you’ll add an Arduino, a popular microcontroller for creating new electronic devices, and integrate sensors to let your robot understand and navigate its environment.


Age: 6 - 13

Duration: 3 months
